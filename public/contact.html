Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FlatFinders

How do I list my property?

Simply create an account as a Property Owner, then click "Post Property" and fill out the listing form. Our team will review and approve your listing within 24-48 hours.

Is FlatFinders free to use?

Browsing properties and sending inquiries is completely free for customers. Property owners can list up to 3 properties for free, with premium options available.

How do I contact a property owner?

Click on any property listing to view details, then use the inquiry form to send a message directly to the property owner. You'll need to be logged in to send inquiries.

What is a Bachelor Flat?

A Bachelor Flat is a single-occupancy rental unit designed for individuals, without roommates or shared living spaces. Use our Bachelor Flat filter to find these properties.

Are properties verified?

Yes, our team reviews all property listings before they go live. We verify basic information and photos to ensure quality listings for our users.

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. We'll send you a link to reset your password within minutes.
