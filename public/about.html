About FlatFinders

We're on a mission to make finding rental properties in Bangladesh easier, faster, and more transparent for everyone.

Our Story

FlatFinders was founded in 2023 with a simple goal: to solve the pain points of finding rental properties in Bangladesh. We noticed that the traditional process of finding a flat was time-consuming, frustrating, and often lacked transparency.

Whether you're a student looking for a bachelor flat near your university, a young professional searching for an apartment in the city, or a family seeking a spacious house - we believe everyone deserves an easy way to find their perfect home.

Today, FlatFinders has grown to become one of the leading property rental platforms in Bangladesh, connecting thousands of renters with verified property owners every month.
